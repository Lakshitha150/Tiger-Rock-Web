01 Introduction

Where Serenity Meets Sri Lankan Stone

Situated in the heart of Sri Lanka's Cultural Triangle, **Dambulla Tiger Rock** is a boutique eco-hotel nestled within ancient granite boulders. Styled with a premium bohemian edge, our retreat offers guests a completely private immersion into nature.

Our highlights are **two exclusive, A-frame lofts** boasting floor-to-ceiling glass windows and cosy mezzanine decks. Step outside your cabin to hike our private stone steps winding directly through giant rocks to reach the summit viewpoint, capturing panoramic sunrises over the forest canopy.
