# Dambulla Tiger Rock - Global Agent Guidelines

## 1. Core Identity & Position
- **Brand Name:** Dambulla Tiger Rock (Do not prefix with "The")
- **Business Profile:** Premium boutique eco-hotel, nature retreat, and 360° climbing-based scenic attraction in Dambulla, Sri Lanka.
- **Key Hook:** A natural elevated summit offering panoramic sunrise/sunset views of the forest, mountains, Sigiriya, and Pidurangala.
- **Tone & Personality:** Natural, peaceful, premium, adventurous, and authentically Sri Lankan.

## 2. Global Branding & Assets
- **Color Palette:** Forest Green, Rock Gray, Earth Brown, Sunrise Gold.
- **Logo Design Direction:** Modern, minimal, nature-inspired. Focus on rock silhouettes, minimal mountain lines, or a rising sun. *Constraint: Avoid literal tiger illustrations unless highly symbolic and subtle.*
- **Typography Style:** Elegant Serif for headers (luxury hospitality feel); clean, scannable Sans-Serif for interfaces, website copy, and POS screens.
- **Primary Tagline:** "Where Sunrise Meets Stone" (Alternatives: "Wake Above the Forest", "Nature. Views. Serenity.")

## 3. Digital Ecosystem Structure
AI agents working across sub-folders must respect these structural requirements:

### A. Core Website Architecture
- **Pages:** Home, Rooms, Experiences, Gallery, Restaurant, About, Contact, Book Now.
- **Future Capabilities Needed:** Online booking API, 360° virtual tour, sunrise notification system.
- **SEO & Google Maps Strategy:** Primary Listing Name must strictly remain **"Tiger Rock Dambulla 360 Sunrise"**. 
- **SEO Keyword Targets:** `Sigiriya view`, `Dambulla sunrise`, `360° panorama`, `rock climb experience`, `sunset viewpoint`, `Sri Lanka scenic viewpoint`.

### B. POS & Internal Systems (Future)
- **Currency:** Must default to LKR (Sri Lankan Rupee) for local tracking with multi-currency conversion options for international card payments.

## 4. Developer & Content Rules
- **Code Standards:** Always write descriptive inline comments for complex calculations, API integrations, and styling states to guarantee smooth handoffs between AI sessions.
- **Social Media Tone:** Warm, inspiring, heavily photography-driven, minimal captions with high emotional impact.
